Design Specs

  • Star Badge (top-right):
    • Position: top: 8px, right: 8px
    • Icon: Material Symbols 'star' (filled)
    • Size: 20px
    • Color: white
    • Condition: photo.rating === 5
  • Video Badge (bottom-left):
    • Position: bottom: 8px, left: 8px
    • Icon: Material Symbols 'play_circle' (filled)
    • Size: 20px
    • Color: white
    • Condition: photo.file_type === 'video'
  • Both badges:
    • Shadow: drop-shadow(0 0 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)) - larger fuzzy spread, lighter opacity
    • Always visible (not just on hover)
    • Non-interactive: pointer-events: none (clicks go through to photo)
    • Can coexist on same thumbnail (starred video shows both)
  • Select Circle (top-left):
    • Position: top: 8px, left: 8px
    • Unchanged from existing design
