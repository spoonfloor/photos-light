Scoreboard Pattern Proposal

Standardize all progress dialogs: total goes in status text, scoreboard shows only category breakdowns or current progress.
THE PATTERN:
• Status text: "{Action} {total} {items}..." (e.g., "Importing 251 files")
• Scoreboard: Category breakdowns ONLY (no total field)
• Benefits: Consistent UX, reduces mental math, cleaner scoreboards
